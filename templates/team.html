This is a list of the wonderful people who keep o!mt up and running and deal with its community.

Community Managers

Community Managers deal with bans, silences, name changes and pretty much everything that has to do with the community.
They take care of our Discord server and reply to emails sent to support@omt.host. Community Managers have a red name in the in-game chat.

{{ template "userGroup" (.Get "badges/members?id=%d" 4) }}

Developers

Developers add new features to the server, squash bugs, keep the server up and running and take care of its maintenance.
They don't do anything related to the community. Developers have a blue name in the in-game chat.

{{ template "userGroup" (.Get "badges/members?id=%d" 2) }}

Chat Moderators

Chat moderators manage the chat to make sure The Law™ (the rules) is respected.

{{ template "userGroup" (.Get "badges/members?id=%d" 30) }}

BATs

BATs play beatmaps in the ranking queue and decide whether they are good enough to be ranked or not.
